On the Freedom Formed Through Knowledge

Education is the movement from darkness to light. It is the ultimate equalizer, a tool that can never be stolen from you once acquired. It gives you the language to argue, the framework to analyze, and the power to build. To be educated is to be deeply un-foolable; it ensures that you see the world as it actually is, rather than how others wish to present it to you. — Allan Bloom
